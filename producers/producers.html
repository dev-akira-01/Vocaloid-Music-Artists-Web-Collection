Producers

The people behind the music. Independent creators who go by P-names — a tradition of adding "P" (short for Producer) to their name. Some started as anonymous teenagers on NicoNico. Some are now industry names. All of them are worth knowing.

DECO*27

Pop · Emotional · Prolific

One of the most celebrated producers in the scene. Known for emotionally charged pop songs that feel intensely personal even coming from a synthetic voice. Later crossed over into writing for human artists, but never left Vocaloid behind. Producer of popular songs like ‐ "Rabbit Hole", "Monitoring", "Hibana", and much more! All featuring Hatsune Miku

PinocchioP

Indie · Experimental · Philosophical

Lo-fi, offbeat, and impossible to categorize. PinocchioP blends absurdist humor with genuine emotional depth — sometimes in the same song. A producer who built his following purely on originality.

Kairiki Bear

Dark Pop · Intense

High-energy, distorted pop built around dark themes — obsession, mania. Songs that get under your skin and somehow stay catchy. Tens of millions of plays and counting.

Kanaria

High-Energy · Dense · Immediate

Arrived with a sound fully formed and the scene noticed fast. Dense layered instrumentals, relentless energy — and a breakthrough song that became one of the fastest Vocaloid tracks to hit 10 million views.
